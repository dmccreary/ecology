# Chapter 17: Evaluating Environmental Claims

## Summary

This capstone chapter applies all prior knowledge to the practical challenge of evaluating environmental claims encountered in social media, news, and everyday life. Students practice identifying misinformation, greenwashing, and conspiracy thinking, and develop fact-checking workflows using source evaluation, statistical reasoning, and constructive skepticism. After completing this chapter, students will be able to systematically evaluate any environmental claim and communicate evidence-based conclusions.
