# Bailey the Beaver — Mascot Style Guide

Meet **Bailey the Beaver**, your guide through this ecology textbook! Bailey is an industrious, curious ecosystem engineer who helps you see how everything in nature is connected.

**Catchphrases:** "Everything's connected!" and "Let's build on that!"

---

## All Mascot Admonition Styles

!!! mascot-neutral "A Note from Bailey"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/neutral.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Bailey in neutral pose">
    This is the **neutral** style, used for general sidebars or introductions
    where no specific emotional tone is needed.

!!! mascot-welcome "Welcome, Builders!"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/welcome.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Bailey waving welcome">
    Everything's connected — and by the end of this chapter, you'll
    see exactly how! This is the **welcome** style, used at the start
    of each chapter.

!!! mascot-thinking "Key Insight"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/thinking.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Bailey thinking">
    Notice how energy flows in only one direction through a food
    web, but matter cycles continuously. Let's build on that!
    This is the **thinking** style, used for key concepts.

!!! mascot-tip "Bailey's Tip"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/tip.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Bailey giving a tip">
    When drawing food webs, always start with the producers at the bottom
    and work your way up to apex predators. This is the **tip** style,
    used for helpful hints and study strategies.

!!! mascot-warning "Watch Out!"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/warning.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Bailey warning">
    Don't confuse food chains with food webs — a food chain is just
    one path through the larger web! This is the **warning** style,
    used for common mistakes and misconceptions.

!!! mascot-celebration "Great Progress!"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/celebration.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Bailey celebrating">
    You've now mastered the basics of energy flow through ecosystems.
    That's a big deal! This is the **celebration** style, used for
    achievements and section completions.

!!! mascot-encourage "You've Got This!"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/encouraging.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Bailey encouraging">
    Biogeochemical cycles can feel overwhelming at first, but
    remember — everything's connected, and once you see the pattern
    in one cycle, the others will click too. This is the **encouraging**
    style, used for difficult content.
